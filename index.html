Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built by anglers, for anglers. No bloat, no paywalls on the basics.

📡

Offline First

Download maps before you head out. Track waypoints, log catches, and view weather — even in areas with zero cell signal. Your data lives on your phone.

🗺️

Depth Maps

NOAA bathymetric charts overlaid on your map. Find the drop-offs, the holes, the structure — the spots where fish actually hide.

🤖

AI Fish Predictions

Powered by weather, water temp, pressure, lunar phase, and your own catch history. Not voodoo — actual pattern recognition.

🔒

Your Data, Your Device

Everything on-device by default. Optional encrypted cloud sync for premium. Fishbrain sells your data — HoneyHole never touches it.

How It Works

From first cast to comeback.

1

Drop a Pin

Mark your honey hole with a GPS waypoint. Add notes, depth, and tags. All saved offline on your phone.

2

Log Your Catch

Record species, weight, length, conditions, bait, and photo. Verified catches get a badge that can't be faked.

3

Come Back Smarter

AI predictions tell you when the fish are biting. Stats show your patterns. The more you log, the smarter it gets.
